Industry Day
21st January 2023

About

The ECE department, IIIT Delhi welcomes you all to be a part of Industry Day 2021, to be held in a hybrid mode. Industry day provides a platform for our industry partners to directly interact with our faculy, students and research scholars to enthuse them about practical research problems, with a focus on building meaningful and long-term collaborations. As a part of Industry Day, interested Industry partners will be expected to present a glimpse of their ongoing research and opportunities for collaborative projects. There will also be presentations from faculty and research scholars followed by a networking lunch. We invite you to partner with us and join us on Industry Day. Please write to us for more details.

Who Should Join

  • » All UG, PG, and PhD students.
